The border-style Property

This property specifies what kind of borden to display:

虛線 A dotted border.

虛線 A dashed border.

實線 A solid border.

雙線 A double border.

凹線A groove border.

凸線A ridge border.

嵌入線An inset border.

浮出線An outset border.

無邊框No border.

隱藏邊框A hidden border.

混合邊框A mixed border.
